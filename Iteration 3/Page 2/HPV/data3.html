Caveats/footnotes:

  • Data extraction based on principal diagnosis ICD-10-AM codes C53 and D06.
  • Periods based on calendar years (1 January to 31 December)
  • Data includes public and private hospitals in all jurisdictions.
  • Rates calculated using ABS backcast population estimates and projections based on the 2016 Census.
  • Age-standardised rates are the number hospitalisations per 100,000 females, age-standardised to the Australian population at 30 June 2001.
  • Data exclude separations for newborns without qualified days, hospital boarders, posthumous organ procurement and admitted patient transfers from another hospital.
  • Source: AIHW analysis of National Hospital Morbidity Database.
